Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day journey through the sacred Dhams

1

Arrival in Dehradun

Gateway to Divinity

  • Arrival: Guests arrive at Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun by 2:00 PM
  • Transfer: Transfer to 5-star / 4-star hotel in luxury AC vehicle
  • Evening: Welcome briefing with refreshments, receive branded duffle bag (5 kg limit)
  • Stay: Overnight at premium hotel in Dehradun
  • Meals: Dinner (multi-cuisine buffet)
2

Dehradun to Yamunotri

Goddess Yamuna Darshan

  • 05:30 AM: Early checkout and transfer to Sahastradhara Helipad
  • 06:00 AM: Helicopter departure to Kharsali Helipad (35-minute flight)
  • 07:00 AM: Arrival at Kharsali, breakfast at luxury guesthouse
  • 08:00 AM: Journey to Yamunotri Temple (6 km trek with palki/pony options)
  • 11:30 AM: VIP darshan at Yamunotri Temple
  • 02:00 PM: Return to Kharsali for lunch and relaxation
  • Stay: Premium guesthouse in Kharsali
3

Yamunotri to Gangotri

Goddess Ganga Darshan

  • 06:30 AM: Breakfast and checkout
  • 07:00 AM: Helicopter departure to Harsil Helipad (35-minute flight)
  • 08:00 AM: Arrival at Harsil and check-in to boutique stay
  • 09:00 AM: AC vehicle transfer to Gangotri Temple (22 km, ~50 minutes)
  • 11:00 AM: VIP darshan at Gangotri Temple
  • 01:00 PM: Return to Harsil for lunch, free time to explore Harsil valley
  • Stay: Overnight in Harsil
4

Gangotri to Kedarnath

Lord Shiva Darshan

  • 07:00 AM: Breakfast and checkout
  • 07:30 AM: Helicopter departure to Sersi / Phata Helipad (40-minute flight)
  • 08:30 AM: Shuttle helicopter to Kedarnath Helipad (10-minute flight)
  • 09:00 AM: Arrival near Kedarnath Temple (500 m walk, pony option included)
  • 10:00 AM: VIP darshan at Kedarnath Temple (one of the 12 Jyotirlingas)
  • 12:00 PM: Return to Sersi/Phata by shuttle, drive to Guptkashi (30 minutes)
  • Stay: Luxury hotel in Guptkashi
5

Kedarnath to Badrinath

Lord Vishnu Darshan

  • 07:30 AM: Breakfast and checkout
  • 08:00 AM: Helicopter departure to Badrinath Helipad (45-minute flight)
  • 09:00 AM: Arrival and hotel check-in
  • 10:00 AM: VIP darshan at Badrinath Temple (Lord Vishnu)
  • Afternoon: Lunch, excursion to Mana Village (India's last village)
  • Evening: Optional temple aarti
  • Stay: Overnight in Badrinath
6

Badrinath to Dehradun

Tour Ends

  • 07:30 AM: Breakfast and checkout
  • 08:00 AM: Helicopter departure to Sahastradhara Helipad, Dehradun (50-minute flight)
  • 09:30 AM: Arrival in Dehradun
  • Transfer: To Jolly Grant Airport or guest's preferred drop-off point
