Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How do I book a Chardham Yatra package?

You can book by filling out our contact form, calling our phone numbers, or visiting our office in Dehradun. Our team will guide you through the booking process.

What is included in the helicopter packages?

Our packages include helicopter transfers, accommodation, meals, priority darshan arrangements, and ground transportation as specified in each package.

Are the helicopter services safe?

Absolutely! We maintain 100% safety record with experienced pilots, regular maintenance, and adherence to all aviation safety regulations.

What is the best time to visit Chardham?

The best time is from May to October when the weather is pleasant and the temples are accessible. We recommend booking in advance for the best availability.
